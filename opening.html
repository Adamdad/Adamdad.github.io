Research研究方向

Research Interests我们关注的问题

01

Generative AI生成式人工智能

Structured generative models such as diffusion models and LLMs for content creation, scene modeling, and understanding.研究扩散模型、LLM 等结构化生成模型，及其在内容生成、场景建模与理解中的应用。

02

Multimodal Learning多模态学习

Fusing text, image, video, and 3D data to build systems that perceive and interact with the physical world.融合文本、图像、视频与 3D 数据，构建能理解并交互物理世界的智能系统。

03

Efficient AI高效人工智能

Model efficiency, optimization, adaptation, compression, and reuse of large pretrained models.关注模型计算效率、优化、适配、压缩，以及预训练大模型的复用。

04

Agentic AI智能体系统

Agents that perceive, plan, collaborate, and autonomously solve complex tasks through modular design.探索可感知、规划、协作的 AI Agent，通过模块化设计完成复杂任务。
