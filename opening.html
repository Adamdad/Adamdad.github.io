研究与荣誉

Research & Honors

我的研究聚焦于生成式 AI、计算机视觉、空间智能、世界模型与高效学习，及其在 3D/4D 视觉与物理世界建模中的交叉应用。相关成果已发表在 NeurIPS、ICML、ICLR、CVPR、ICCV、ECCV 等人工智能与计算机视觉领域的顶级会议与期刊，并入选 AAAI 2026 New Faculty Highlight，获得 NeurIPS 2022 最佳论文提名奖、Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia、2023 国家优秀自费留学生奖学金、2024 世界人工智能大会青年优秀论文提名奖、百度奖学金 Finalist 等多项荣誉。 My research focuses on Generative AI, Computer Vision, Spatial Intelligence, World Models, and Efficient AI, particularly their application to 3D/4D vision and physical world modeling. My work has been published in top-tier venues, including NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CVPR, ICCV, and ECCV, and has received several honors, such as the AAAI 2026 New Faculty Highlight, NeurIPS 2022 Best Paper Honorable Mention, Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia, and the 2023 Chinese Government Award for Outstanding Self-Financed Students Abroad.

💡 研究方向 💡 Research Interests

选择一个长期且有意义的研究方向并不容易。我希望我们的研究能聚焦于人工智能的核心问题，努力构建更聪明、更高效、更能理解和交互物理世界的AI系统。主要方向包括： Our research aims to tackle fundamental challenges in AI by building smarter and more efficient systems that can understand and interact with the physical world. Key directions include:

  • 生成式人工智能（Generative AI）：研究结构化生成模型（如扩散模型、LLM），及其在可控生成、内容生成、场景建模与理解中的应用。 Generative AI: Investigating structured generative models (e.g., Diffusion Models, LLMs) for controllable creation, content generation, scene modeling, and understanding.
  • 计算机视觉与空间智能（Computer Vision & Spatial Intelligence）：融合图像、视频与 3D/4D 数据，构建能理解、重建并推理物理世界的智能系统。 Computer Vision & Spatial Intelligence: Fusing images, videos, and 3D/4D data to build intelligent systems that can understand, reconstruct, and reason about the physical world.
  • 高效人工智能（Efficient AI）：关注模型计算效率、参数/能耗优化，以及预训练大模型的压缩、适配与重用。 Efficient AI: Focusing on the computational efficiency, optimization, and adaptation of large pre-trained models.
  • 世界模型与具身智能（World Models & Embodied AI）：探索能够生成、重建、推理并与动态物理世界交互的智能系统。 World Models & Embodied AI: Exploring intelligent systems that can generate, reconstruct, reason about, and interact with dynamic physical worlds.

这些方向互为支撑：🤖 生成模型提供通用创造能力，🌐 视觉与空间智能扩展其对现实世界的理解，⚡ 高效学习确保模型实用落地，🧠 世界模型与具身智能最终连接感知、生成、推理与行动。 These areas are interrelated: 🤖 generative models provide general creative capabilities, 🌐 vision and spatial intelligence enhance understanding of the real world, ⚡ efficient AI ensures practical deployment, and 🧠 world models connect perception, generation, reasoning, and action.

如果你有其他感兴趣的、有潜力的新方向，也欢迎和我交流探讨，我始终对此保持开放态度。 I am always open to discussing new and exciting research ideas.
